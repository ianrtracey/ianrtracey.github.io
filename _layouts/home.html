Hi there! My name is Ian Tracey, a software engineer from Boston, MA now living in Tucson, AZ.

I believe in the potential software has to shape how we work, live and play. I am with obsessed with finding challenging problems and creating immense value out of the solutions.

I work at Latitude where I design cloud APIs and backend systems for drone operations, mapping and imagery platforms. Previously I have worked on moving money through the internet at Braintree and an enterprise social network at Cisco.

Besides writing code and designing systems, I love discovering great food and coffee, running hackathons spending (too much) time on Spotify,exploring new cities with friends and learning new tools and technology.
