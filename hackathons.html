Building world-class hacker culture in the southwest

I go to a lot of hackathons after starting Hack Arizona, a 800+ student hackathon based at the University of Arizona.

Hackathons are near and dear to my heart and over the years I have been humbled by the enthusiasm and inspiration that comes out of the hacker community. I have learned a ton and it has truly been a privledge to serve the community for over the past 3 years as I work to help foster world class hacker culture in the southwest.

I must now pass the torch to the next generation of organizers at Hack Arizona who I believe will do an even better job at expanding the conversation of what technology will look like in the southwest in the next decade. It has been one hell of a ride and by far one of the most rewarding things I have ever done.
